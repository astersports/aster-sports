FINDING 2 — F-B1-MONEY: registration_fees and family_balances are DISCONNECTED
   (the RD-4 reconciliation is unbuilt — material to T1 AND to PR-B1)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EVIDENCE (live this turn):
  - family_balances viewdef reads ONLY financial_accounts (season_fee_cents,
    discount_cents) + financial_transactions (payment/refund/adjustment/fee).
    It has ZERO reference to registration_fees.
  - submit_registration writes guardians, players, player_guardians, registrations,
    registration_fees, player_equipment — and NOT financial_accounts /
    financial_transactions (verified function body).
  - triggers on registrations/registration_fees: only set_updated_at. No
    reconciliation to financial_accounts.
  => a NEW funnel registration creates a registration_fees row but ZERO
     family_balances delta. The two money systems are disconnected (RD-4 "half-
     built reconciliation", confirmed live).
  - LH pilot today: imported families HAVE financial_accounts (family_balances
     works for them); registrations=0, so no funnel family exists yet. The gap is
     LATENT until the first funnel registration — which is exactly what Track B
     turns on.

IMPACT ON THE RATIFIED CONTRACT:
  - D-G4 T1: "authoritative_total_cents == family_balances delta for that account"
    is UNPROVABLE — a registration produces no family_balances delta. The provable,
    correct assertion is: authoritative_total_cents == sum(registration_fees) for
    the new registration(s). The family_balances clause should be DROPPED from T1
    (or replaced by an assertion that family_balances is UNCHANGED, documenting the
    disconnect).
  - PR-B1: the spec has the parent's "what you owe" come from family_balances
    (and RD-3 A4 has the admin detail read family_balances too). For a funnel-
    registered family that's $0/absent — so B1 would show a family that just
    registered (and owes the fee) as owing NOTHING, while their registration_fees
    say otherwise. That is PATTERN A #63 re-introduced: two sources for "what this
    family owes" (registration_fees vs family_balances), the precise bug the
    redesign exists to kill.

THE FORK (architect ruling needed before PR-B1):
  (1) BUILD THE RECONCILIATION (architect-lane, the clean #63-collapse): on a
      registration, create/update the family's financial_accounts row (e.g.
      season_fee_cents += the registration total, or post a 'fee'
      financial_transaction) so family_balances becomes the ONE source of "owes"
      for funnel AND imported families. This is the RD-4 contract finally built.
      Likely a small SECDEF step inside (or after) submit_registration + a
      migration. CC LEAN — it makes family_balances the single money source the
      whole redesign assumes, and unblocks B1's balance display honestly.
  (2) B1 reads registration_fees for funnel families, family_balances for imported
      — REJECT: two sources = the #63 regression; contradicts RD-3 A4.
  (3) DEFER funnel-family balance: B1 shows registration STATUS only (no $ owed)
      for funnel families until the reconciliation lands; imported families still
      show family_balances. Interim, but "what you owe" is a headline B1 goal, so
      this ships B1 half-built.
  CC LEAN: (1). It's architect-lane (migration + maybe an RPC step). If (1) is
  in, it likely becomes PR-B0 (reconciliation) BEFORE PR-B1, and the G4 test then
  CAN assert the family_balances delta (closing the loop the contract wanted).
