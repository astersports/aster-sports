EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
The parish's DATA is healthy; the site's PRESENTATION and IA are the problem.
Mass times, confession times, bulletins (current to April 2026), and staff
contacts are accurate and consistent with every third-party directory checked —
there are no P0 wrong-facts errors. The liabilities are: a primary nav carrying
year-stamped URLs (2021/2022-2023) for programs that are mostly STILL ACTIVE
(the staleness is in titles/URLs, not the ministries); no "I'm new here" path;
online giving that exists (WeShare) but is nearly undiscoverable; an active
YouTube livestream channel the site doesn't feature; a Teen Life page whose one
contact action renders broken; and a CYO page frozen at 2017-18 while actual
registration lives on LeagueApps/Blue Sombrero. A Wix rebuild can fix all of
this and preserve SEO via Wix's URL Redirect Manager (301s from every legacy
path). Estimated migration effort: 40-60 hours (manual; eCatholic has no bulk
export).

E. DESIGN LANGUAGE (Window-flyer palette, WCAG-corrected)
---------------------------------------------------------
Palette: hunter green (~#355E3B) / gold (~#D4AF37) / ivory (~#F5F5DC),
charcoal #333 for body text.
WCAG gates (verified ratios):
  - Gold on ivory FAILS (~2:1) — never use for text.
  - Ivory on hunter green PASSES (~8:1) — primary header/CTA combo.
  - Gold on hunter green ~3.5:1 — large headings (18pt+/14pt bold) only.
  - Charcoal on ivory PASSES (~18:1) — body text.
Usage: green+ivory section blocks; gold as accent (borders, icons, hover
states, large display headings on green). Serif display + sans body. Real
parish photos over stock (youth pages especially). Buttons: ivory text on
green, gold border on hover.
