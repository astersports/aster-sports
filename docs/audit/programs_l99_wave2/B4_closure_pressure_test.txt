  but pre-checks the WRONG SCOPE vs the live constraint. checkSlugAvailable filters

  org A owns, then hits a raw 23505 global-unique violation on UPDATE/INSERT. E1

  SCOPE. The closeout never flagged this (it predates the multi-org lens being
  applied to the slug path). NEW corroboration: the E1 fix and the slug-scope bug
  coexist in the same 4 lines.

  first paint (fire-and-forget async). This is the SAME shape as AP#44 ("trace the

  the dialog renders against pre-fetch state. For the P0 it doesn't matter (counts
  never carry financials), but it means the closeout's "B1 fixed the stale count"
  claim is OPTIMISTIC: the race is narrowed (self-corrects within a render), not
  closed. To truly close: await the count and gate the dialog open on the fresh
  value, or render a loading state in the confirm.
