ASK 2 — THE TWO FACTS FOR THE PR-B0 LEDGER ENCODING (architect rules)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FACT (i) — family_balances balance formula (exact, from the live viewdef):
  billed_cents   = season_fee_cents - discount_cents          (per financial_account)
  net_paid_cents = SUM over financial_transactions of:
                     payment    -> + amount_cents
                     refund     -> - amount_cents
                     adjustment -> + amount_cents
                     fee        -> - amount_cents
  balance_cents  = billed_cents - net_paid_cents
  => a posted 'fee' transaction of amount A: net_paid -= A, so balance += A. A 'fee'
     INCREASES what's owed. (season_fee_cents can stay 0 and the 'fee' transaction
     alone drives the balance — OR the charge can live in season_fee_cents. Both
     reach the same balance; that's the encoding choice below.)

FACT (ii) — financial_accounts grain + uniqueness (live):
  UNIQUE (org_id, guardian_id, season_id)  -- ONE account per family-guardian per
                                              program (season_id FK -> programs,
                                              ON DELETE RESTRICT).
  guardian_id nullable; season_fee_cents/discount_cents default 0.
  financial_transactions: account_id FK (CASCADE), org_id, transaction_type (text),
  amount_cents (NOT NULL), occurred_at (NOT NULL), processing_fee_cents default 0;
  recorded_by/applied_to_player_id nullable. No uniqueness on transactions (append-
  only ledger).

CC's READ + LABELED LEAN on the encoding (architect rules the final form)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The single-source principle is fixed (family_balances). Two live-grounded encoding
choices remain for the architect:

  ENCODING A (CC LEAN) — append-only 'fee' transaction, account as a pure shell:
    On each genuinely-new registration (v_reg_id IS NOT NULL, i.e. after the ON
    CONFLICT so a duplicate posts nothing — exactly T1c):
      1. ensure financial_accounts (org_id, guardian_id:=primary guardian,
         season_id:=program) via INSERT ... ON CONFLICT (org_id,guardian_id,
         season_id) DO NOTHING; season_fee_cents stays 0.
      2. post ONE 'fee' financial_transaction on that account, amount_cents :=
         sum(registration_fees for THIS registration) [= base + add_on, and the
         family_discount line (negative, on reg[1]) is already inside that sum so
         the family nets correctly across its per-reg 'fee' transactions].
    Why CC leans here: append-only matches the existing ledger shape (payments/
    refunds are transactions); idempotent by construction (no new reg -> no fee);
    keeps season_fee_cents meaning "admin-set season price" untouched for imported
    families; registration_fees stays the itemized receipt, family_balances the owes.
    Family-cap discount needs NO special mapping — it's already a negative
    registration_fee line folded into the per-reg sum.

  ENCODING B — charge lives in season_fee_cents/discount_cents (no 'fee' txn):
    On registration, set account.season_fee_cents += gross fee and discount_cents
    += family_discount. Reaches the same balance, but: it mutates the same column
    admins edit for imported families (mixes funnel + admin intent), it's not
    append-only (re-derivation on dup is fiddly), and the family-cap maps to
    discount_cents (a second place discount can live). CC does NOT lean here.

OPEN for the architect to RULE (the encoding, per the lane): A vs B; if A,
  per-registration 'fee' (CC lean) vs per-fee-line 'fee' (more granular rows);
  and confirm the account keys on the PRIMARY guardian (v_guardian_id), co-guardian
