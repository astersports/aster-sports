HEADLINE
The class Frank caught three times is real, and it is structural: ride and
volunteer data has ZERO hook-level gate enforcement anywhere in src/. Every
gate that exists lives at an individual render site, so every NEW consumer
of those tables re-creates the contradiction by default. That is why fixing
the card (round 6) did not fix Home. Four user-visible P0s: the two Home
signal hooks (the screenshots), the detail page's own Rides section gating
on only HALF the chain, and the rsvp-nudge resolver emailing families whose
kids cannot RSVP. Beyond the P0 class: 7 fix-now P1/P2 findings, 9
catalogued P2/P3, and 6 agent claims DISCARDED as false positives after
CC verification (listed — they are not silently dropped).

F-1  P0 | src/hooks/useRideNeeded.js (entire hook)

     neither org feature_settings.rides_enabled nor event.enable_rides.

     also no academy-eligibility filter on its (event x kid) candidates.
     Contract: D3 gate chain. Fix: gate at hook entry via the shared

F-2  P0 | src/hooks/useVolunteerSlots.js (entire hook)

     Contract: D3 gate chain (volunteers = org toggle only).

F-3  P0 | src/pages/EventDetailPage.jsx:121
     The Rides section gates on the org toggle ONLY:
       org?.feature_settings?.rides_enabled !== false && <CollapsibleSection
     Missing: event?.enable_rides === true. This is the DETAIL half of the
     screenshot: the section renders (org toggle on), opens, and the tab
     inside reports "Ride coordination is off for this event"
     (EventRidesTab:23 reads the per-event flag). A section whose only
     content is "this section is off" should not render.
     Contract: D3 — "rides surface ANYWHERE <=> org AND event."
     Fix: full chain at the section gate (one line) via the shared helper.

F-4  P0 | src/lib/engine/resolvers/rsvpNudge.js:33-46
     fetchUnrespondedSlices builds the nudge recipient set from ALL active
     team_players with no eligibility filter: for a GAME, unactivated
     academy kids land in `unresponded`, so their guardians receive nudge
     EMAILS asking them to RSVP for a game where the app (correctly)
     renders them no control. totalRoster is inflated by the same rows, so
     the nudge copy's denominator diverges from every card. This is the
     email-lane sibling of B2 (usePendingRsvps), which was already fixed.
     Contract: SD-6 eligibility. Fix: read player_activations for the
     event when event_type is game/tournament and drop unactivated academy
     rows from `active` before computing unresponded/totalRoster. Resolver
     is web-side only (the edge-function tick computes only the going-count
     threshold, which unactivated kids cannot affect) — no AP#30 mirror
     change needed; verified.
