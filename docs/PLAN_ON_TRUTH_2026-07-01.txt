PART A — THE AAU PARENT EXPERIENCE (design plan)
=====================================================================

WHO THIS PARENT IS
Not a Tuesday-rec parent. AAU = logistics under stress, punctuated by pride: a
schedule that dropped Wednesday night, three pool games Saturday across two gyms,
a bracket seed unknown until other teams finish, a 7:40 tip = leave at 6:55, a
second kid on another team, a grandparent asking "did they win?" Lived in a loud
gym, one bar of signal, one hand, two-second glances. Judged in the first 30
seconds of the first Saturday on one question: calm and in control, or behind?

THE NORTH-STAR PROMISE
By Sunday night, a parent never once had to wonder where to be, when to leave, or
whether their kid's team was still in it — and at least twice, the app gave them
a reason to feel proud. Calm as the floor. Pride as the ceiling.

FIVE PARENTS, ONE HOME, ZERO CONFIGURATION
The home self-assembles around THEIR family (tracked teams + their kids):
  • Logistics Parent (majority) — where/when/court/leave-by → the Up-Next hero.
  • Proud Parent — score/result/who's-next/clip → pride surfaces itself.
  • New/Anxious Parent — what happens, what to bring → first-run warmth.
  • Multi-Kid Parent — two teams, one brain → one merged agenda, never two apps.
  • Remote Family (grandparent/away) — follow along → live scores now, film later.
  • (cross-cutting) Spanish-speaking Parent — full EN↔ES parity, never second-class.

  ASSEMBLY MUST BE LEGIBLE, NOT MAGIC. AAU is multi-kid/multi-team, so the
  assembly has more ways to guess wrong than a single-subject app — a silent
  wrong-kid-first makes the Anxious Parent MORE anxious (confident-wrongness in
  UI form). Degrade gracefully: when assembly is uncertain (new user, ambiguous
  multi-kid) show an obviously-correct plain "here's your day"; make the hero
  LEGIBLE — the parent sees WHY this game is first (next chronologically), one
  tap to the other kid. Legibility beats cleverness.

DESIGN PRINCIPLES (gym-reality, explicit)
  1. Glanceable in 2s, one-handed. Next game + when-to-leave is the hero, always,
     above the fold, thumb-reachable. Everything else one tap deeper (§16.14).
  2. TWO NOTIFICATION LANES — do NOT put a human in the path of a time-critical
     fact.
       • FACTS (court/time change, clinch) — SYSTEM-SENT, immediate, TRUTH-gated.
         The truth gate already prevents fabrication, so admin-gating a CONFIRMED
         fact adds latency without adding safety — a 6:40am court change that
         waits for an admin can reach the parent at 6:55, after they left for the
         wrong gym. Leave-by is the paid headline; nothing human in its path.
       • MESSAGES (social/discretionary — "great game today") — admin-DRAFTED,
         reviewed, sent. Calm-by-default; never floods the parent.
  3. Honest or silent — never confidently wrong. Exact where we have it;
     "Schedule releases Wednesday" over a fake time; no invented odds until
     calibrated. Silence beats fabrication. A design value, not just eng.
  4. Pride is engineered, not hoped — and OBEYS THE TRUTH GATE. "You're in the
     bracket" fires on CLINCHED (monotonic exact) ONLY — never on projected/
     likely. A false "you're in!" is a trust-killer dressed as joy, worse than no
     pride moment. Team-level + engagement only, never per-kid game stats (§16.12).
  5. Works on one bar of signal. Fast first paint, tiny payloads, cached
     last-known truth (the 52KB→3KB work is this principle).
  6. Progressive depth — paywall the SOLUTION, never the PROBLEM. The existence
     of a two-gyms-overlapping-times conflict is a FREE honest truth the parent
     must see ("you can't be in two places — here's the overlap"). The travel-
     math that RESOLVES it is Plus. Hiding the collision behind the wall violates
     "free logistics = the trust deposit." Free shows the collision; Plus routes.

SURFACE PLAN (tiered by trust)
FREE — "I always know where to be" (anxiety killer / trust deposit):
  • Up-Next Hero — next game · opponent · court · time · weather · one-tap
    directions. The whole app in one card (AauUpNextHero exists; make it the
    unmistakable center of gravity).
  • Self-assembling family agenda — legible per above; one team or four merged.
  • Live scores + honest bracket — Playoff Path before it's played, honest TBD
    for unresolved seeds, never a fabricated matchup.
  • Track-from-search, synced across devices — tap Track, it follows you on your
    account (the #1172 fix is a DESIGN requirement: losing a tracked team loses
    faith).
  • Findable from a Google search — indexability = part of the experience.
  • HANDOFF PRIMITIVE (free, acquisition) — one-tap READ-ONLY share of a tracked
    team's schedule/scores. AAU parents hand off mid-tournament constantly
    ("watch game 3, I'll be back for the bracket"); the shared-to adult lands on
    the Hub, tracks, converts. Schedule/score is public data → safe + frictionless.
    Film share stays guardian-gated.
PLUS — "I leave at the right minute, and the weekend just works" (paid calm):
  • Traffic-aware leave-by — real traffic, not distance÷speed. "Leave by 6:52" is
    the most valuable sentence you can hand an AAU parent at 6:40am.
  • Multi-game / multi-gym travel-conflict ROUTING (the collision itself is free
    per principle 6; the routing is Plus).
  • Schedule-change alerts that feel like a heads-up from a friend — but delivered
    on the FACTS lane (immediate), not gated behind an admin draft.
PREMIUM — "I got to see it, and so did Grandma" (shareable pride):
  • Family Film Room — clips, guardian-gated, jersey-not-face, one-tap delete.

TRUST & SAFETY AS FELT EXPERIENCE
Kid privacy is visceral to an AAU parent. The design SHOWS the guardian gate,
never invents a stat/matchup, never floods the phone (two-lane model), makes film
consent + deletion one obvious tap. The parent should feel — without being told —
that the app treats their kid the way they would.

INCLUSION (table stakes, not a phase)
Full EN↔ES parity — PARITY OF TONE, not just coverage. The honest-state and pride
microcopy ("Schedule releases Wednesday," "clinched," "TBD seed") is emotional
register, not literal words — it needs a bilingual copy pass by someone who feels
both, or the Spanish-speaking parent gets the calm floor without the warmth (a
subtly second-class path). Plus VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, ≥4.5:1 contrast, 44px.

SATISFACTION SIGNALS
  • First-Saturday retention · zero "where do we go?" texts in the team thread ·
    track-rate · time-to-answer ~2s · Free→Plus conversion after a good weekend.

PART C — THE ARCHITECTURE
=====================================================================
THESIS: one backend, one ingest, one truth engine, one entitlement writer —
every guarantee enforced at the DATA layer, never the UI — feeding progressively-
enhanced clients across three trust planes. The pilot is TENANT #1 of a multi-
tenant system, not a single-tenant app to generalize later.

THE SPINE — four invariants, true at the database regardless of client:
  1. Security boundary = RLS + SECURITY DEFINER helpers, NEVER the client. anon
     reads only the public-read RPC surface; everything else guardian-gated;
     canAccessChild false even for super-admin, DB-enforced. A client is a
     renderer with zero authority.
  2. Money is a closed write-path. Client never writes entitlement. ONE writer is
     the sole mutator of family_entitlements, accepting TWO independently-signed
     sources (Stripe + Apple ASN), each validated. Super-user OR's into the CHECK.
  3. Truth flows ONE direction: server RPC → client render. No client tally/
     advancement/odds math. "Exact-or-silent" is a data contract, not a UI choice.
  4. Schema owner-applied + mirrored. Schema is the system of record; code conforms.

THE SEAMS — where the system is deliberately cut:
  1. PUBLIC_RPC_CONTRACT — THE KEYSTONE. The frozen interface between the truth
     engine and THREE consumers: web SPA, serverless prerender, native app.
     Freeze once → truth port + indexability + native all build against a stable
     surface. This is the critical path, not a task.
  2. Anon/authenticated seam — free wedge (anon+web+prerendered, indexable, no
     PII) vs paid/child half (authenticated+native). Drawn at the RPC boundary.
     Why the web-first build isn't wasted; why a binary can't be the wedge.
  3. Prerender seam — serverless fn fetches ONLY anon RPCs, returns crawlable
     HTML, SPA hydrates. Architecturally INCAPABLE of reaching guarded data.
  4. Entitlement seam — read is_entitled everywhere, write only via the one
     service; two sources behind one writer so web+native never hold two "paid"
     truths. Keep it a Supabase edge fn, service-role write, explicit user_id
     param — do NOT reintroduce client-JWT minting (flagged smell; never in money).
  5. Origin seam — localStorage (anon, origin-scoped) vs account-synced tracking;
     the R2 cross-origin 301 strands origin-scoped anon state → G1 handoff is an
     architectural gate, not an afterthought.

EVOLUTION — single pilot → multi-tenant, no rewrite: schema is org_id-scoped with
FK-inherited tenancy today; RLS helpers are tenant-aware. Tenant #2 differs on
data, not structure. What becomes tenant-customizable is governed by the two-
bucket config doctrine: CONSUMPTION is the gate — build a config surface only when
a shipped surface reads it or multi-tenant onboarding needs it; else hardcoded-
and-dormant.

FAILURE CONTAINMENT — blast-radius isolation (each irreversible change its own
reversible step; cutover before money; money-paths serialized); money
observability (alert on any non-writer write); fail-loud honesty (broken artifact
visible in smoke, not silent in a parent's inbox); atomic rollback on Vercel
(host follows runtime; no Railway question while serverless).

OPEN FORKS (architect frames; owner ratifies) — with leans:
  1. Prerender: on-demand serverless (LEAN — live content, dynamic routes) vs SSG/ISR.
  2. Entitlement writer host: edge fn service-role (LEAN) vs anything minting client JWTs.
  3. Traffic vendor: decide on cost-per-request at R3 volume (LEAN), not features.
  4. RBAC: land restrict-down as real roles BEFORE R5 (LEAN — tenancy defect otherwise).
  5. Scout return tier: keep OFF the free indexable Hub (LEAN) until guarded like .io.
  6. Native submission: only when R3 paywall (IAP-aware) + R6 child posture ready (LEAN).

THE KEYSTONE: freeze the RPC contract. It unblocks the truth port, the prerender,
and the native client in ONE act — the one decision whose absence forces three
consumers to chase a moving target.
