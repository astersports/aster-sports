4. DECISIONS AWAITING ARCHITECT RATIFICATION (forks)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
F1. Predictor strength model (#1125 + #147). Choices baked in, ratify or
    recalibrate:
      - Rating = opponent-adjusted average point margin, one SOS pass, per-game
        margin capped ±25, aggregated by resolved_key across all public
        tournaments. Null when a team has no finals anywhere.
      - Win prob = logistic(0.1 * rating_gap)  (10-pt edge ≈ 73%).
      - Grade prior = 12 rating points docked per grade a team is listed below
        its division floor (heuristic; a team that plays up is occasionally
        elite — Frank accepted this knowingly). Grades parsed from names; absent
        grade → neutral.
      - Gate: no precise % until "results" or a strength/grade signal exists;
        otherwise "—".
    Open calibration question for the architect: are 0.1 (scale) and 12
    (grade penalty) the right magnitudes once real Grand Finale games start
    landing? They were tuned to (a) sane spreads and (b) putting the 4th-grade
    team clearly last — not yet against post-game outcomes.

F2. Google sign-in is auth (architect-scoped per §2.4). Shipped under Frank's

    owner-only on tracked_teams (#1124, full CRUD owner-scoped, UNIQUE(user_id,
