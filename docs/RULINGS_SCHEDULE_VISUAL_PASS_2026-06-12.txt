V0 PRINCIPLE (the test for every rule below): a parent glances for THREE
facts — when, where, is-my-kid-set — and the card answers without reading.
Everything else is supporting cast: smaller, grayer, or absent. The deployed
card failed not on data but on HIERARCHY: five same-weight text rows + four
competing color systems. The fix is structure, not decoration.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
V1 — CARD SURFACE: kill the cobalt wash. RULED.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every card is WHITE (--as-bg-card) with the team-color left stripe. Game
emphasis = 6px stripe (vs 4px practice) + the GAME/TOURNAMENT type tag —
never a background wash. The wash made games read as selected/disabled
states, fought the team color, and crushed chip contrast. One exception
survives: the NOW slot's accent ring (V6). Cancelled/completed dim via
opacity (unchanged from the spine).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
V2 — TWO TYPE FAMILIES, EACH WITH ONE JOB. RULED.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PILLS (borderRadius 999) carry exactly one thing: TIME-STATUS, right of the
time — countdown ("in 23h"), "Happening now", "Today". Nothing else is ever
a pill on this card.
CHIPS (borderRadius 6, 11px/700, soft bg) carry FACTS in one row below the
meta line: going/denominator (green soft), rides/volunteers needed (amber
soft), covered (neutral soft, check), academy status (academy soft, V5).
W/L result chip (completed games) sits in the time-row right slot — it
REPLACES the pill slot, same geometry as the countdown it supersedes.
Weather is plain 12px tertiary text ("☀️ 74°"), right of time, before the
pill — it is ambient, not a badge.
The inline "Tournament/Practice" type label: 11px/600 UPPERCASE tracking
+0.05em tertiary, sitting right-aligned ON THE META LINE (not glued to the
title). At compact it appears only when the title doesn't already say it
(practice cards say "Practice" as the title; game cards carry the tag).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
V3 — COMFORTABLE ANATOMY ("Detailed"): the planning view. RULED.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Six slots, 4px grid, padding 14px 16px (stripe side 16px):
  1 TIME ROW      17px/700 time · right: weather + status pill (or W/L chip)
  2 TITLE         15px/600 "vs. Atlantic Sharks" (opponent-first; tournament
                  anchors show the tournament name) — 2-line clamp
  3 META LINE     13px: team dot + team name (team color, 500) · kid name
                  (secondary, 600) · right-aligned TYPE tag (V2)
  4 LOCATION      13px secondary, pin icon, tappable (accent) when mapped
  5 FACTS CHIPS   the V2 chip row + own-commitment line (accent soft)
  6 RSVP          parent+upcoming only: TWO 44px buttons side by side —
                  [✓ Going] [Can't make it] — quiet outline, going fills
                  green-soft when selected. Live events: the 12px lock line
                  "RSVP closed". Notes render here at comfortable only.
Approximate height: 150-170px with RSVP; ~120px without.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
V4 — COMPACT ANATOMY: the glance view. STRUCTURALLY DIFFERENT. RULED.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Three slots, padding 9px 12px:
  1 GLANCE ROW    15px/700 time + 14px/600 title ON ONE LINE (title
                  truncates) · right: weather + status pill
  2 META LINE     12px, ONE truncating line: team dot · team · kid · "·"
                  location (no pin icon at compact)
  3 FACTS CHIPS   same V2 row (the §10.2 operator floor — chips stay) +
                  commitment inline at row end when short
  RSVP (parent+upcoming): the 44px SEGMENTED Going/Can't control, full
  width — the one tall element compact keeps (tap-target floor).
Approximate height: ~88px with RSVP; ~64px without. The pair now differs
by STRUCTURE (3 slots vs 6) and ~2x height — visibly distinct at arm's
length, which was the entire complaint.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
V5 — ACADEMY TREATMENT: out of the RSVP slot. RULED.
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
An unactivated academy kid on a game card renders an ACADEMY CHIP in the
facts row — "Milo · Academy" (academy soft/violet, 11px) — and NO RSVP
control, no full-width pill. The old treatment put a violet pill where the
RSVP lives, which read as a broken control three cards in a row (Frank's
stack). Activated academy kids get the normal RSVP control and count in
the denominator (SD-6, unchanged). Comfortable adds the quiet explainer
under the chips: "Not activated for this game" 12px tertiary — compact
omits it (the chip is enough at a glance).
