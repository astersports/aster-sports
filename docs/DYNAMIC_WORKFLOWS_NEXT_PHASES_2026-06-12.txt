a. Session open: the hook runs the pre-flight automatically; CC reads WARNs
   and reconciles before new work (no manual checklist execution).
b. Build: small PRs by surface; every PR created -> ready -> auto-merge armed
   in the same burst (§15) -> subscribe_pr_activity immediately. CI failures
   and review comments arrive as events; CC fixes event-driven, no polling.

   event -> AskUserQuestion to Frank rather than guessing (the same pattern
   that routed D-a..D-f as decisions instead of unilateral calls).

   CC arms ~1h re-checks until PRs are MERGED/CLOSED (webhooks do not cover
   CI-success / merge-conflict transitions). NOTE: send_later was absent in
   this session's environment — subscriptions still cover comment/CI-failure/
   review events; the gap is logged here per AP #62 (interim, expires when
   the tool ships in this environment).
