🔴 IAP GATE (design in from the start — do NOT retrofit): if Hub Plus sells inside the iOS

  rejects. IAP makes Apple App Store Server Notifications a SECOND upstream source of
  "this account is entitled." RECONCILE the money gate precisely, don't abandon it:
  keep ONE entitlement-writer service that accepts TWO SIGNED upstream sources — Stripe
  events AND Apple server notifications — each validated; client never writes. Gate
  becomes "one writer, two trusted sources."
  [VERIFY at R3 design] external-purchase-link / steering rules are in flux (2024 DMA +
  2025 US injunction on external links) — pull Apple's CURRENT policy before building the
  paywall; it may change whether a Stripe-link path is even permitted.
Delivers North Star: 06 $20/account + super-user; the Plus tier of 01/02.
