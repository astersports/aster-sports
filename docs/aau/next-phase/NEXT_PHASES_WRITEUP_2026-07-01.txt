ASTER SPORTS — THE NEXT PHASES
A strategic write-up of the road from a free tracking Hub to a paid, multi-tenant platform.
CC lane · 2026-07-01 · grounded against astersports/aster-sports @ origin/main
Canonical plan: docs/aau/next-phase/AAU_HUB_FULL_PHASED_APPROACH_2026-06-30.txt (architect).
This document is the narrative on top of that plan — the "why it's shaped this way," for the

=====================================================================
WHERE WE STAND (grounded)
=====================================================================
The free, no-login AAU Hub is live at astersports.app/hub. A parent can search any AAU team,
open a tournament, drill into a division (standings / schedule / bracket), open a team, track
teams (anonymously, or synced to a free account), and see an up-next countdown with game-day
weather. Behind it sits one Supabase backend, one ingest path, and a family of anon public
RPCs that are the frozen read contract. Auth, identity, and password recovery are hardened.
Foundation (R0) is substantially done and R1's insulated half (PR-A) has shipped.

That is the beachhead. Everything that follows is about converting it — deliberately, one
irreversible step at a time — into a product families pay for and programs run on.

=====================================================================
THE THESIS
=====================================================================
One product, two clients, three planes of value.
  • One product: astersports.app, one backend, one truth engine feeding every surface.
  • Two clients: the WEB (all tiers, and the only SEO-indexable surface — the free wedge must
    live on the web) and a NATIVE iOS companion (for the authenticated tiers, where push and
    camera actually earn their keep).
  • Three planes: T0 Free Hub (no login, indexable, forever free) → T1 Hub Plus ($20/account)
    → Program B2B (register + pay, org-scoped).

The growth model is product-led. We do not buy the top of the funnel; we EARN it. A parent
googling "\<team\> schedule" lands on a fast, honest, free Hub, tracks their kid's team, and —
only after we've proven we can tell them what court and what time — is offered a $20/month
assistant that removes Saturday-morning logistics pain. Programs adopt the platform because
their families already live on it. Premium (film, AI) is the last, highest-margin layer, sold
to families who already trust us with the basics.

The discipline that makes this real is SEQUENCING BY BLAST RADIUS: never run two irreversible
changes at once. Cut the front door over once, early, on a proven Hub. Prove one money-path in
production before opening the next. Gate child data and video behind the strictest controls and
build them last. Each phase retires exactly one class of risk before the next begins.

=====================================================================
THE THREE STANDING GATES (the spine — they hold at every phase)
=====================================================================
🟢 TRUTH  — We show exact facts where we have them and stay SILENT where we don't. Standings,
            records, and "what it takes to make Sunday" are exact math. A win-probability %
            is SUPPRESSED until the model earns calibration (a real Brier score) on live
            results — and a clinched team never shows a probability at all. Labels track the
            math. Silence before fabrication. This is the product's credibility, and it is a
            gate, not a preference.
🔴 MONEY  — Exactly ONE entitlement-writer service is the sole writer of who is paid. It accepts
            TWO signed upstream sources — Stripe events and (for in-app purchases) Apple App
            Store Server Notifications — each validated. The client never writes entitlement.
            The owner/super-user unlocks reads by OR-ing a role flag into the CHECK, never by
            writing. Checkout is test-mode-first.
🔴 CHILD  — Anything involving a minor is guardian-verified only. canAccessChild stays false
            even for the super-admin. This invariant is true from today, so the R6 film tier
            slots into a posture the whole platform has honored all along — not a bolt-on.

=====================================================================
THE PHASES
=====================================================================

R1 · FREE HUB (T0) — finish the wedge  [PR-A shipped · PR-B + indexability open]
  Goal: the consolidated free Hub, proven by real traffic before anything else moves.
  Two remaining bodies of work:
    • PR-B (truth port): standings, records, bracket, advancement, and the honest-odds/exact-
      math surfaces render from FROZEN RPCs, and the client-side tally/advancement math is
      DELETED in the move. This is where today's known standings quirks die — not patched,
      retired. GATE: the owner applies the held standings/bracket substrate to prod and freezes
      PUBLIC_RPC_CONTRACT.md first. PR-A does not wait on it; PR-B cannot start without it.
    • Indexability: the Hub is currently a client-rendered SPA — one generic title, empty
      pre-JS body, no real sitemap/robots. The entire SEO wedge is unbuilt. The fix is a
      serverless per-route prerender on Vercel (NOT build-time SSG — content is live and routes
      are many) that sources the same anon RPCs and emits crawlable HTML + unique per-route
      title/description/canonical/OG, plus a real sitemap and robots. Sequence smallest-blast
      first: robots + sitemap + per-route meta, then crawlable body; standings-in-HTML inherits
      the PR-B freeze. This is unblocked and buildable now.
  Exit: a real-traffic canary at parity with the old Hub, the old Hub frozen, and representative
  routes indexing at parity. Risk retired: "is the free surface good enough, and can Google see
  it?"

R2 · FRONT-DOOR CUTOVER — consolidate to one home, once  [sequenced]
  Goal: make astersports.app the canonical front door BEFORE money ever enters. 301 the legacy
  surfaces (astersports.io/aau + legacy-hoopers) route-by-route to their .app equivalents;
  freeze and retire the old surfaces; .io becomes the marketing platform (the gold portal +
  register CTA). Stays on Vercel — the atomic un-301 rollback IS the safety net; no host swap.
  Exit gates (do not flip until all pass): a tested un-301 rollback; a route-level 301 map (not
  a homepage dump); index-at-parity; and — critically — an anonymous-tracking handoff, because
  localStorage is origin-scoped and a naive cross-origin 301 would strand every anonymous user's
  tracked teams. Risk retired: "consolidate without losing SEO or stranding users, reversibly."

R3 · HUB PLUS (T1) — the first dollar  [sequenced · money-gated]
  Goal: the platform's first consumer money-path, plus the contained "the weekend works"
  assistant. 7-day trial → $20/mo per ACCOUNT (not per kid, not per team — "one account, every
  kid, every team"). Plus unlocks: traffic-accurate leave-by + directions + game-time weather,
  schedule-change alerts with conflict/split-up detection, and the Saturday-night bracket model.
  Two hard pre-reqs, designed in from the start, not retrofitted:
    • A traffic vendor (TomTom / Google Routes) locked before R3 — a trafficless leave-by on a
      Saturday morning is just distance÷speed, wrong often enough to drive first-weekend churn,
      and leave-by is the headline thing a parent pays for.
    • The IAP gate: if Plus sells inside the iOS app, Apple generally requires StoreKit IAP for
      a digital subscription. That makes Apple server notifications a SECOND signed source into
      the one entitlement writer — reconcile the money gate to "one writer, two trusted sources,"
      don't abandon it. [Verify current Apple external-link policy at R3 — it is in flux.]
  Exit: a parent starts the trial from the free Hub, the single-game leave-by is traffic-accurate,
  they convert to $20/mo, consent + pre-charge reminder are verified, and entitlement gates clean
  in production. Risk retired: "can we charge money correctly and safely, once."

R4 · HUB PLUS COMPLETENESS (T1 depth)  [sequenced]
  Goal: extend the proven assistant without holding the first paid launch hostage to it. Native
  push (the first capability that truly justifies going native — reliable background push beats
  web push, especially on iOS); multi-game weekend agenda and cross-venue travel conflicts over
  the same traffic vendor; schedule-change diffs. And the odds calibration (A4): once a Brier
  score is earned on real results, the weighted % UN-suppresses and shows LABELED beside the
  exact conditions. Until earned, exact count/status only. Risk retired: "deepen value and let
  the estimate speak — only after it has earned the right."

R5 · PROGRAM LADDER (B2B)  [sequenced · needs real RBAC]
  Goal: programs adopt the platform to maintain content and run registration. Claim/verify a
  listing → a Program Hub (schedules, rosters, scores, announcements) → registration + payments.
  The non-money parts parallelize freely; CHARGING waits until the consumer money-path (R3) is
  proven in production. Hard pre-req: real role-based access control before any cross-org data —
  "super-admin first, restrict-down" must land its restrict-down here. Risk retired: "other orgs'
  data, safely isolated, with a second money-path proven behind the first."

R6 · PREMIUM · FILM · AI (verified tier)  [last · hard-gated]
  Goal: the evergreen premium layer and the biggest net-new build. A family film room with
  grounded AI review — "it reviews the footage, it never invents it" — jersey-not-face, consent
  + one-tap deletion, verified-guardian only, COPPA-gated so canAccessChild stays false for
  everyone including the super-admin. Native camera / photo-library capture is the second
  capability that justifies the app. This is deliberately last: video ingest, frame-by-frame
  face-minimization ML, secure media storage, and streaming are a different engineering
  discipline with their own vendor and cost model, and Apple's kids-privacy review is strictest
  here. Until R1–R5 are operational and the entitlement writers are processing real revenue, Film
  stays a DESIGN CONTRACT and a grayed-out "soon" tab — a promise in the UI and the pitch, not a
  line of backend code. Risk retired: "the highest-liability, highest-margin build, attempted
  only from a position of proven trust and revenue."

PARALLEL LANE · APPLE / NATIVE iOS  [start enrollment now]
  Not a release in the sequence — it runs alongside. The one thing to start immediately: Apple
  Developer Program enrollment (org enrollment needs a D-U-N-S number + identity verification and
  can take weeks — it is the slow administrative step, and it blocks nothing technical). The
  native app BUILDS after R2, is IAP-aware from R3, and gains its two justifying capabilities at
  R4 (push) and R6 (film capture). Submit only when the R3 paywall and R6 child posture are
  review-ready; a complete, compliant build clears Apple review faster than a shell.

=====================================================================
THE CRITICAL PATH (what unblocks what)
=====================================================================
  1. OWNER applies the held standings/bracket substrate + freezes PUBLIC_RPC_CONTRACT.md
     → unblocks R1·PR-B (the truth port; mockups 02/04 leave their honest-held state).
  2. Indexability build (no gate) proceeds in parallel with (1) → unblocks the R2 index-at-parity
     exit gate.
  3. R1 canary at parity → unblocks R2 cutover.
  4. Traffic vendor locked + entitlement writer built → unblocks R3.
  5. R3 proven in production → unblocks R5 charging.
  6. Real RBAC → unblocks R5 B2B.
  7. Apple enrollment (start now) + R3 paywall + R6 posture → unblocks App Store submission.
Everything else is sequenced behind these. The single highest-leverage operator action today is
(1): it is the gate on the truth port, and the truth port is what makes the Hub credible enough
to charge for.

=====================================================================
WHY THIS PLAN IS RIGHT
=====================================================================
It wins the free wedge before charging, cuts over once and reversibly, proves one money-path
before opening the next, and gates the highest-liability build (child video) to last — attempted
only after trust and revenue exist. The truth gate protects credibility, the money gate protects
the ledger, the child gate protects the most vulnerable users, and all three hold at every phase.
The substrate and the gates already hold today; what remains is to walk the sequence without
skipping a step. Ship, don't gate by config; prove, don't assume; exact where we know, silent
where we don't.
