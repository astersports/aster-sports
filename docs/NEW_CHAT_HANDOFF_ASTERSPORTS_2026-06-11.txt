==============================================================================

  CC (Claude Code): implements + verifies against ground truth (file:line, execute_sql,
    deployed state); produces committed artifacts WITH evidence + a labeled LEAN (AP#39).

§11.8 ARCHITECT COLLABORATION (all 6):
  1. Every cross-lane artifact = a committed plain-text .txt in docs/ PLUS a full chat
     paste PLUS delivered as a file. No backtick-wrap of the whole doc; never a
     summary-instead-of-paste; never deferred. The doc IS the deliverable.
  2. Division of labor: CC verifies + surfaces evidence + a labeled lean; architect
     frames/rules; Frank ratifies. CC never settles a fork alone.
  3. Round-trip is doc-to-doc: architect questions arrive as a doc; CC answers with an
     evidence-grounded .txt (Frank's questions carried verbatim inside CC's doc).
  4. Forks ratified before a phase CLOSES: CC writes the close-out review (audit +
     open forks + triage); architect ratification IS the close.
  5. Every session ends with a committed CC_SESSION_HANDOFF_<date>.txt (this kind of doc).
  6. Architect DECISIONS go in a committed ARCHITECT_DECISION_REQUEST_<date>.txt:
     per item REQUEST / EVIDENCE (file:line or query) / OPTIONS+REQUIREMENTS / CC LEAN.
     Batch multiple. NOT for pure verification asks or status recaps.
