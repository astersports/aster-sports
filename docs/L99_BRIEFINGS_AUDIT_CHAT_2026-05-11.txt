composed in Resend payload at send time.

Fix: per-kind resolver in send-tournament-message:
     - weekly_digest → "Legacy Hoopers · Week Ahead"  (Frank's locked choice)
     - game_recap, rsvp_nudge → "Coach {first_name} · Legacy Hoopers"
     - schedule_change → "Coach {first_name} · Legacy Hoopers"
     - tournament_prelim, tournament_recap → "Coach {first_name} · Legacy Hoopers"
     - academy_callup_notice → "Coach {first_name} · Legacy Hoopers"
     - other/unknown kinds → fall back to organization_settings.from_name

Source org name from organizations table or org_settings, NOT hardcoded.
