Context sets the order, not a rule

"Team first or type first" is three different questions. The admin's order is set by where they started. The coach has no order at all. The parent never composes, so their "order" is the order they read, which is the whole three-second test.

The only kind-first path
Why kind-first only on the cold path: the kind decides which audiences are valid and what the smart default is, so "recap" can pre-fill "this team." Pick a team first from cold and the form still can't shape itself until you also pick the kind, so it just adds a step. Everywhere you have context, you get team-first for free.
2

Coach

No order to choose. The coach triggers from where they already are; it lands in your Radar pre-filled.
A game becomes "request recap." A team becomes "send update." Team and kind are inferred from context, the coach's job ends at the tap, and you own the words. Simplest possible, and it holds the trigger-only line.
