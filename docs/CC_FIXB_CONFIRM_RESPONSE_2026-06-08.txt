1. §5 CONFIRM ANSWERED (ground truth)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q: Does the composer know the recap's scope (event_type), or must it derive from
   the recap's events? Can a recap mix scopes?

A: DERIVE from the recap's events. Evidence:
   - game_recap (singular) ALREADY selects event_type (gameRecap.js:33).
   - games_recap (plural) does NOT yet (gamesRecap.js:21 EVENT_SELECT) — CC adds
     `event_type` to that select as step 1 of the build.
   - A recap CAN mix scopes AND teams: the picker is useRecentPlayedGames(orgId)
     (GamesRecapBody.jsx:37), which lists ALL recently-played published games
     (last 60 days) across every team and every event_type; the admin
     multi-selects freely with no team/scope constraint (GamesRecapBody.jsx:41
     toggle). So mixed-scope and multi-team selections are both reachable.

2. REFINEMENT (extends F2's guard to the F3 pill — needs your nod)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The plan says F2 gates the season FACT to single-team, but F3b says the season
pill applies "on BOTH single- and multi-game recaps." Those agree for the
common case but not for multi-TEAM / mixed-SCOPE recaps, where a single
season-to-date number is undefined. CC's implementation rule (consistent with
F2, just stated for the pill too):

   Season-to-date applies (BOTH the F4 fact AND the F3b pill) only when the
   recap is SINGLE-TEAM and SINGLE-SCOPE (all selected events share one
   event_type). "Multi-game" in F3b means many games of ONE team in ONE scope
   (e.g. a 10U Blue league weekend) -> season pill YES.

   FALLBACK when multi-team OR mixed-scope:
     - no season_summary fact (level A guardrail governs the prose), AND
     - the record_pill stays today's recap-window behavior (summarizeGames
       recordPill) — honest for a cross-team/cross-scope digest.

This keeps F3b coherent and never shows a wrong/blended season number. If you'd
rather multi-team show per-team season lines now, that's F2b (v2) — CC defers.
